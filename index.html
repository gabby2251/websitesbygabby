FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a website cost?

Every project is different. Contact me for a free quote — I'll ask a few questions about what you need and get back to you quickly.

Do I need a booking system?

No. If you already use Acuity, Square, Calendly, or another service, I can integrate it into your website so your customers can book without leaving your site.

Will I own my website?

Yes. Your website belongs to you. I build it, hand it over, and you're in full control.

Do you provide updates after launch?

Yes — 30 days of minor updates are included after launch. Need something changed after that? Just reach out and we'll figure it out.
