How It Works

Getting a website is easier than you think

You don't need to know anything about websites. I handle everything. Here's what the process looks like:

1

You reach out

Text me, email me, or send me a message on Facebook. Just say "I need a website" and we'll go from there. No pressure, no commitment yet.

2

I send you a simple form

Once we chat, I'll send you a short form to fill out — things like your business name, what you do, your hours, and any photos you want to use. You fill it out at your own pace.

3

I build your website

I take care of everything — the design, the setup, making it work on phones. You'll get to see it before it goes live and request any changes.

4

You pay & go live

Once you're happy with it, you pay and your site goes live. I accept CashApp, Zelle, and Venmo. After launch, you get 30 days of small updates included.

FAQ

Questions I get a lot

Do I have to know anything about websites?

Nope! That's my job. You just tell me what your business does, send me your info and photos, and I handle the rest. I'll walk you through everything in plain language.

How much does it cost?

Starter websites are $150. Business websites are $250. Custom projects start at $400. You pay once — no monthly fees, ever.

How do I pay?

I accept CashApp, Zelle, and Venmo. I typically ask for half upfront and half when the site is done — but we can talk about what works for you.

Will I own my website?

Yes. The website is built specifically for your business and belongs to you. I handle all website updates and changes, so if you ever want something added, removed, or updated, just reach out to me. Small updates are included free for 30 days after launch, and affordable update options are available afterward.

What if I already use a booking app like Square or Calendly?

No problem — I can add your booking link right into the website so customers can book without leaving your page.

How long does it take?

Most websites are done within 1–2 weeks once I have your info. The biggest factor is how quickly you can fill out the form and send me your photos.

What if I want changes after launch?

Small changes are included free for 30 days. After that, small updates are $20, and new sections or pages are $50+.
